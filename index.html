Why Should We Eat Healthy Food?

Healthy eating helps maintain a healthy weight, reduces the risk of chronic diseases like heart disease, diabetes, and cancer, and promotes overall health and well-being.

Why Should We Not Eat Unhealthy Food?

Unhealthy foods, especially those rich in sugars, fats, and processed ingredients, can lead to weight gain, increase the risk of chronic diseases, and impact your mental well-being.
